Exercise 8.

Sound travels about 750 miles per hour. If you stand in a parking lot near a building and sound a horn, you will hear an echo.

  1. Suppose it takes about ½ a second to hear the echo. How far away is the building?

  2. Now let’s generalize that result. Suppose it takes \(n\) seconds to hear the echo. How far away is the building, in terms of \(n\text{?}\)
