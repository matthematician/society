Suppose you count 80 beats in a minute. To convert this beats per year:

\begin{equation*} \frac{80\text{ beats}}{1\text{ minute}}\cdot\frac{60\text{ minutes}}{1\text{ hour}}\cdot \frac{24\text{ hours}}{1\text{ day}}\cdot \frac{365\text{ days}}{1\text{ year}} = 42,048,000\text{ beats per year}. \end{equation*}
